Other Ways to Help

  1. SUPPORT US WHEN SHOPPING AMAZON.COM

    2. Make purchases under Amazon Smile and a portion of your order will be donated back to Riverside Art Center. It's easy to sign up and easy to support RAC! Go to smile.amazon.com and choose Riverside Art Center to support.

  2. KROGER REWARDS PROGRAM

    3. Give to RAC by going to Kroger rewards site and sign in. Then go to My Account in the upper right-hand corner, click on that & edit the Kroger Community Rewards button, put in the number for Riverside: 49146, then click on the circle for Riverside and you're done! It's really easy and RAC benefits.

  3. COMMUNITY MARKET'S CASH PROGRAM

    4. If you have Community Market receipts, please bring them to Riverside Art Center, Community Market will support us with 2% of your non-acholic purchases. These funds support supplies to sustain our art center. Thank You for supporting Riverside Art Center!
